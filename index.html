Igniting stories
since first spark

Fable & Flame was born from a love of honest cooking — where open fire meets refined technique. Our kitchen champions local farms, wild catch, and heritage grains.

Chef Margot Varela brings years of Michelin-starred experience to this intimate space, but you’ll find no pretension here — only the joy of sharing unforgettable meals around a crackling hearth.

Every plate tells a story: from the ember-roasted beets to the slow-braised lamb shoulder. We believe dining is ceremony, and you are our honored guest.

— Chef Margot & team
